## Overview

Take control of a frog as you leap, swim, and swing your way through serene, nature-filled levels in **PondQuest**, a mixed reality platformer featuring innovative hand tracking gameplay. Explore a lush pond ecosystem teeming with life, collect flies, and master acrobatic moves as you climb ever higher through vibrant, vertical environments. Can you make it to the top?

## Key Features

- **Hand Tracking Gameplay** - Intuitive controls using Meta Quest's advanced hand tracking technology
- **Mixed Reality Experience** - Seamlessly blends virtual pond environments with your physical space
- **Vertical Platforming** - Climb, leap, and swing through beautifully crafted nature-filled levels
- **Immersive Ecosystem** - Explore a living, breathing pond world teeming with life
- **Acrobatic Movement** - Master frog-like abilities including jumping, swimming, and swinging

## Platform

Available in **Early Access** on Meta Quest
