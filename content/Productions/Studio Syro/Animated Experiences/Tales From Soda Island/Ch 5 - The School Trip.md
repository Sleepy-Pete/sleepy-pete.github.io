## Overview

Behold the marvels of Sodian science! Follow a single drop of glucose rain on a transformative journey across the island as the complex ecosystem processes it into a deliciously sweet carbonated beverage. Available in many flavors or all of them at once.

## Extras

**Tales from Soda Island Extras!**

Watch more content about the world of Soda Island:

- **Fragments of Knowledge**: Hold your breath and dive into the Underwater Library. Discover new fragments of knowledge about the Soda Island ecosystem and its inhabitants. Contents: Soda Brewing Process, Glucose Rain, Pygmies, and Silence.
- **Behind the Scenes**: Take a peek behind the curtain and see some of the concept art, storyboards, and visual exploration that goes into building the world of Soda Island.
- **Soda Flavors**: Visit the Sodian Natural Science & History Museum and explore the different flavors of Soda produced on the island.
