## Overview

Continue your journey on Soda Island. Embark on an adventure through the Neon Jungle as you follow a Tadpolotl on its quest to deliver a package through the twisted depths of the jungle.

## Extras

**Tales from Soda Island Extras!**

Watch more content about the world of Soda Island:

- **Fragments of Knowledge**: Hold your breath and dive into the Underwater Library. Discover new fragments of knowledge about the Soda Island ecosystem and its inhabitants. Contents: Tadpolotl, Neon Jungle, Pygmies, and Silence.
- **Behind the Scenes**: Take a peek behind the curtain and see some of the concept art, storyboards, and visual exploration that goes into building the world of Soda Island.
- **Tadpolotl Lifecycle**: Visit the Sodian Natural Science & History Museum and get an up-close and personal look at the different stages of the Tadpolotl lifecycle. Please no tapping on the glass.
