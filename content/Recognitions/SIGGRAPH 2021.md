# SIGGRAPH 2021

**VR Theater - Tales From Soda Island: The Neon Jungle**

## Recognition

Tales From Soda Island: The Neon Jungle (Chapter 2) was selected for the VR Theater program at SIGGRAPH 2021, one of the world's premier conferences on computer graphics and interactive techniques.

**SIGGRAPH History Archives**: [Tales From Soda Island: The Neon Jungle](https://history.siggraph.org/experience/tales-from-soda-island-the-neon-jungle-by-fourgnier-franke-ariet-ladd-steif-et-al/)

## About Tales From Soda Island: The Neon Jungle

Chapter 2: The Neon Jungle is part of the Tales From Soda Island series, a Meta-published VR animated series created entirely in Quill. This chapter takes viewers on a vibrant journey through a neon-lit world filled with unique characters and visual storytelling.

**Studio:** Studio Syro  
**Platform:** Meta Quest

## About SIGGRAPH

SIGGRAPH is the premier international conference and exhibition on computer graphics and interactive techniques. The VR Theater program showcases cutting-edge immersive experiences and demonstrates the latest advances in virtual reality technology and artistic expression.

## Significance

This selection for SIGGRAPH's VR Theater program recognized Tales From Soda Island as a landmark achievement in VR animation and demonstrated the potential of Quill as a professional production tool for creating immersive narrative experiences.
