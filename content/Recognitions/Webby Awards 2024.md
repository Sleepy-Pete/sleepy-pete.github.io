# Webby Awards 2024

**Nominee - Best Narrative Experience (Immersive Features)**  
**Silence: Tales From Soda Island, Chapter 6**

## Recognition

Silence: Tales From Soda Island, Chapter 6 was nominated for Best Narrative Experience in the Immersive Features category at the 2024 Webby Awards.

**Webby Awards Entry**: [Silence: Tales from Soda Island - Ch. 6](https://winners.webbyawards.com/2024/ai-immersive-games/immersive-features/best-narrative-experience/280504/silence-tales-from-soda-island--ch-6)

## About Silence: Tales From Soda Island, Chapter 6

Chapter 6: Silence is part of the Tales From Soda Island series, a Meta-published VR animated series created entirely in Quill. This chapter explores themes of communication and connection in the whimsical world of Soda Island.

**Studio:** Studio Syro  
**Platform:** Meta Quest

## About Webby Awards

The Webby Awards are the leading international awards honoring excellence on the Internet, including websites, interactive media, online advertising, mobile sites and apps, social media, virtual reality, and podcasts.

## Significance

This nomination recognizes the narrative excellence and immersive design of Silence, affirming the importance of storytelling in VR experiences and the innovative approaches being developed in spatial narrative design.
