# SXSW 2025

**The Art of Change**

## Recognition

The Art of Change was featured at SXSW 2025, one of the largest and most influential film, interactive media, and music festivals in the world.

**SXSW 2025 Film Lineup**: [View on SXSW.com](https://sxsw.com/festivals/film/lineup/)

## About The Art of Change

The Art of Change is an immersive album experience created in collaboration with DROELOE, bringing music to life through spatial storytelling and hand-painted VR animation. The project was created entirely in Quill, a revolutionary VR illustration and animation tool.

**Directors:** Simone Fougnier (Funilab) and Vincent Rooijers (Droeloe)

## About SXSW

SXSW (South by Southwest) is a film, interactive media, and music festival and conference held annually in Austin, Texas. It is known for showcasing innovative and groundbreaking work in film, technology, and music.

## Significance

This recognition at SXSW 2025 demonstrates the growing importance of immersive experiences in contemporary storytelling and the impact of innovative approaches to music visualization and narrative design.
