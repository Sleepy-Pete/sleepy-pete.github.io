# Mediterranean International Film Festival (2024)

**Best VR Works - Tales From Soda Island, Chapter 7**

## Recognition

Tales From Soda Island, Chapter 7 received the Best VR Works award at the Mediterranean International Film Festival in Malta in 2024.

**Awards Announcement**: [Mediterrane Film Festival 2024](https://2024.mediterrane.com/mediterrane-film-festival-closes-second-edition-with-golden-bee-awards-ceremony/)

## About Tales From Soda Island, Chapter 7

Chapter 7: The First Ingredient is part of the Tales From Soda Island series, a Meta-published VR animated series created entirely in Quill. This chapter was also selected for the 80th Venice International Film Festival.

**Studio:** Studio Syro  
**Platform:** Meta Quest

## About Mediterranean International Film Festival

The Mediterranean International Film Festival is held annually in Malta and celebrates innovative and groundbreaking work in cinema and immersive media, with a focus on Mediterranean culture and contemporary storytelling.

## Significance

This award recognizes the artistic excellence and technical achievement of Tales From Soda Island, Chapter 7, affirming its position as a leading work in VR animation and immersive storytelling.
