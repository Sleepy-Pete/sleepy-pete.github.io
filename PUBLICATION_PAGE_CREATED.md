## Key Information Extracted

### Paper Details:
- **Title**: Creating a Universe from Scratch: Studio Syro's Handcrafted VR Pipeline
- **Authors**: Peter Ariet and Nick Ladd
- **Conference**: SIGGRAPH 2025 - Spatial Storytelling Track
- **Date**: August 10-14, 2025
- **Location**: Vancouver Convention Centre, Vancouver, BC, Canada
- **DOI**: 10.1145/3721244.3742456

### Abstract Summary:
The paper documents Studio Syro's approach to creating animated stories entirely within virtual reality using Quill. It covers:
- The production pipeline for Tales from Soda Island (first VR-animated series)
- Expansion to other projects (Nyssa, The Art of Change, music videos, stage visuals)
- Interactive experiences (PondQuest, Dear Metaverse)
- VR animation workflow, Unity prototyping, and artist-friendly tools
- Building worlds with small teams

### Topics Covered:
1. **VR-Native Production**
   - Quill as primary animation tool
   - Hand-painting and animating in VR
   - Spatial storytelling techniques

2. **Projects**
   - Tales from Soda Island
   - Nyssa (Reimagined Volume I)
   - The Art of Change
   - PondQuest
   - Dear Metaverse
   - Music videos and stage visuals

3. **Technical Approach**
   - VR creation workflows
   - Unity prototyping
   - Small team methodology
   - Handcrafted aesthetic

### Conference Context:
SIGGRAPH 2025 Spatial Storytelling track featured diverse presentations on:
- Mixed reality theater
- AI-driven spatial audio
- Virtual puppetry
- Interactive XR installations
- Aquatic VR experiences
- Live performance in VR
